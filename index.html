About PlumbCo

Founded in 2005, PlumbCo started as a small, family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and high-quality plumbing services to our community. Over the years, we've grown, but our core values of integrity, customer satisfaction, and innovation remain unchanged.

We believe in using the latest technology to solve plumbing problems efficiently and effectively. Our team of licensed and insured plumbers is dedicated to continuous training, ensuring we're always at the forefront of the industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all non-emergency services. For emergencies, we have a standard dispatch fee which is waived if you proceed with the repair.

What should I do in a plumbing emergency?

First, shut off the main water valve to your house to prevent further damage. The valve is usually located in the basement, a utility closet, or outside near the water meter. Then, call us immediately for 24/7 emergency service.
